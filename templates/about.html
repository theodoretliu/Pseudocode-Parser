Our Goal: Make Coding Simpler

When first learning to program, ideas, not syntax, are of utmost importance. Our goal is to make coding simpler, and in doing so, bring code to more people. No more getting bogged down by syntax. No more messing with indentation. Simply say what you mean to say, and see the code generated right in front of your eyes. Not happy with the results? Shoot us an email at pseudocodeparser@gmail.com. We are happy to help! We hope you enjoy this product.
